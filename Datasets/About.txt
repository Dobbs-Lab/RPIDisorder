###############Explanation of Datasets###############
There are 6 datasets included in this distribution.

The training datasets (RPI2241):
1. Positive:
	Positive_Fasta_RPI2241.txt
2. Negative:
	Negative_Fasta_RPI2241.txt

Testing datasets (RPI12252):
3. Positive:
	NPInter_UniqSeq.txt 
4. Negative dataset:
	NRBP_pairs.txt

FMRP interactions:
5. Positive:
	fmrp_positive_interactions.txt
6. Negative FMRP interactions:
	fmrp_negative_interactions.txt
	
	
All files are in the (.rpi) format:

>Heading
PROTEINSEQUENCE
RNASEQUENCE

The RPI2241 dataset can be found here:
Muppirala UK, Honavar VG, Dobbs D. Predicting RNA-Protein Interactions Using Only Sequence Information. BMC Bioinformatics. (2011) 12:489 
DOI: 10.1186/1471-2105-12-489
https://bmcbioinformatics.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/1471-2105-12-489

The RPI12252 dataset was created by Rasna Walia.
The publishing of this dataset is pending.

The positive interactions for the FMRP dataset come from:
Darnell JC, Jensen KB, Jin P, Brown V, Warren ST, Darnell RB. Fragile X Mental Retardation Protein Targets G Quartet mRNAs Important for Neuronal Function. (2001) 107(4):489-499
DOI: 10.1016/S0092-8674(01)00566-9
http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0092867401005669
